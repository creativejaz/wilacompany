OUR STORY

We are Wila + Co.

From our kitchen to a home studio, we proudly create eco-conscious, clean-burning candles designed with your health in mind.

meet the team

Michelle: Visionary + Scent Alchemist

“As a child, I would often mix perfumes, lotions, and soap with sand + crayon shavings for fun. Getting lost in the wonder of what I would create, envisioning myself as an alchemist brewing up a fascinating creation. Who would have known I was honing my skills for my passion.” - Michelle

Kids: Cheer Squad

The "company" in Wila + Company. A creative bunch of individuals, whose support helps keep us going. We use entrepreneurship as a form of education and like to include them in the process. As an extension of Wila + Co, our kids are creating a collection and we can't wait to share it with you.

what we do

All of our candles are made with an in-house blend of natural coconut wax and soy wax, making them non-toxic, vegan, skin-safe, and biodegradable. The wooden wicks we use are made from FSC Certified Wood, hand-crafted + made in the USA. We infuse all of our scent blends with clean fragrance oils and organic essential oils. This enhances the quality of the aroma and the overall experience of our candles. 

We're determined to help you find a balance between creating your ideal sanctuary, while also reducing any impact on the environment. A candle where luxury meets sustainability.

SUSTAINABLE INGREDIENTS

That's why we only use sustainable natural ingredients, and why we've developed a unique method for creating Candle Refills. We don't want you to have to buy a new candle jar every time you want to enjoy a Wila + Co. Candle. We encourage you to repurpose your candle jar or refill it. Learn more about our Refill Program!
